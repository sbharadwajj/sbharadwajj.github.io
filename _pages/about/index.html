I am a full-time research intern at the Perceiving Systems Department at Max-Planck Institute of Intelligent Systems, Tübingen, Germany. I am advised by Michael Black and Victoria Fernández Abrevaya.

My research interest lies in the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics. I am particularly interested in reconstructing 3D models from 2D images.

I completed my masters in Machine Learning at the University of Tübingen and I worked in the Autonomous Vision Group for my masters thesis, supervised by Andreas Geiger. My thesis focused on improving 3D reconstruction of static urban environments by exploring the benefits of depth information for radiance fields.
