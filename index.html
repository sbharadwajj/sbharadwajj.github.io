Shrisha Bharadwaj

I am a Ph.D. student advised by Michael J. Black and Victoria Fernández Abrevaya at the Max-Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, Tübingen. My research interest lies in generative neural rendering, specifically, in relighting. I have worked on inverse rendering and 3D reconstruction in the past. I am currently exploring if relighting can be achieved through generative neural rendering without explicitly modeling the geometry, material and lighting.

I completed my masters in Machine Learning at the University of Tübingen and I did my my masters thesis at Autonomous Vision Group on improving the underlying 3D representation of NeRFs.

Note: I am searching for an internship starting summer/fall 2026!
